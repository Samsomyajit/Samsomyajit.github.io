Trust@Health: A Trust-Based Multilayered Network for Scalable Healthcare Service Management

Trust@Health models healthcare relationships across multiple organizational layers to support interpretable trust-based ranking and scalable service management.

Publisher DOIarXivProject page

Plain-language summary

Healthcare decisions involve connected relationships among doctors, departments, hospitals, and patients. Trust@Health represents these relationships as a multilayer network and uses trust signals to rank services in an interpretable way.

Research contribution

  • Multilayer modeling of healthcare entities and service relationships.
  • Trust-based ranking across connected network layers.
  • Scalable analysis validated against real-world ratings.
