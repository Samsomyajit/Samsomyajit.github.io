CenterDistill

Weakly supervised knowledge distillation for ambiguity-aware cross-lingual question answering.

Publisher DOIPublication pageRelated language-model research

Research problem

Cross-lingual question answering becomes difficult when labeled data are limited and the meaning of a question or candidate answer is uncertain.

Main contributions

Weak supervision, teacher–student learning, and ambiguity-aware evaluation for cross-lingual QA.
